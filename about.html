About Us

Inspired by the esoteric and absurd, our collection of puppets is unlike anything you've ever seen before. Each puppet is meticulously created with basic tools, loads of effort, and boundless passion.

They are not just puppets; they are expressions of the unconventional and offbeat ideas that thrive on the fringes of the karmic grid.
